Which Music accompanies this dance?

The musical backdrop creates a mood or atmosphere for the dance that will color how the audience sees and experiences it.

How do i dance as a beginner?

Get inspired,warm up and stretch,learn basics and grooves.take dance classes.

What dance should i learn?

The best recommendation is to start with Rumba,foxtrot,swing,cha cha and salsa.

Ladies can choose Fitted tops e.g tank tops or crop tops with comfortable bottoms like leggings or dance skirts For Men a well fitted shirt along with dance pants or slick.
